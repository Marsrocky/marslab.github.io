Dr. Shan is a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Nanyang Technological University, working with Prof. Jianfei Yang. His research interests include force- and tactile-aware robot learning, multimodal vision-language-action models, generalizable manipulation, robotic dynamics modeling, contact-aware manipulation, and embodied AI. More information can be found on his [personal webpage](https://shanshilin.github.io/)
